Ulitmate Frisbee

All about Ultimate Frisbee:

Ultimate was developed in 1968 by a group of students at Columbia High School in Maplewood, N.J.

It combines the non-stop movement and athletic endurance of soccer with the aerial passing skills of football, played by two teams with a flying disc on a field with end zones with the object to score by catching a pass in the opponent's end zone.

All about Quilting:

Quilting is the process of sewing two or more layers of fabric together to make a thicker padded material - typically done with three layers.

The earliest known quilted garment is depicted on the carved ivory figure of a Pharaoh ~3400 BC and a quilted floor in Mongolia dating between 100 BC to 200 AD.
